Privacy Policy for Triple Riddle

Effective date: May 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Triple Riddle handles information about users and their devices.

App developer: MidiSG
Privacy contact: midisoft.game@gmail.com

1. Information We Collect and Process

Triple Riddle does not require users to create an account and does not ask users to provide their name, phone number, address, or other direct contact information.

The app may process the following types of data:

  • App usage data, such as game starts, game completions, hint usage, settings changes, shop interactions, purchase restoration, and result sharing actions.
  • Crash and diagnostic data, such as crash reports, stack traces, app state at the time of a crash, device model, operating system version, and other technical information.
  • Device data and identifiers that may be processed by third-party SDKs, including advertising identifiers, app instance identifiers, IP address, device model, operating system version, and language settings.
  • Advertising data, including ad impressions, ad interactions, consent choices, and data needed to display, measure, and improve ads.
  • Purchase-related data, such as purchase status, purchase identifiers, and information needed to provide purchased features. Payment details, such as card numbers, are processed by Google Play or Apple and are not received by Triple Riddle.
  • Local app data, such as game progress, statistics, settings, language preference, purchase state, token balance, notification preferences, and reminder schedule.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's core features.
  • Save game progress, statistics, settings, language choice, and purchases.
  • Show ads and provide rewards for watching ads.
  • Process in-app purchases and restore previously purchased features.
  • Send local reminder notifications if the user enables notifications.
  • Analyze app usage and improve the user experience.
  • Diagnose crashes, fix bugs, and improve app stability.
  • Prevent abuse and keep services working correctly.

3. Advertising and Consent

Triple Riddle uses Google AdMob to display ads. Google AdMob may collect and process device information, advertising identifiers, IP address, ad interaction data, consent choices, and other technical information needed to serve, personalize, limit the frequency of, and measure ads.

Where required, the app uses Google's User Messaging Platform to request and manage advertising consent and privacy choices. On iOS, the app may also request App Tracking Transparency permission before using data for tracking across apps and websites owned by other companies.

Users can manage ad personalization through the app's privacy choices, their device settings, and Google account settings.

More information about how Google uses data from apps and sites that use its services is available here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

4. Analytics and Crash Reporting

Triple Riddle uses Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used and Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports. These services may process technical device data, app usage events, crash information, and app instance identifiers.

This information helps us find issues, improve app stability, and understand which features may need improvement.

5. In-App Purchases

In-app purchases in Triple Riddle are processed through Google Play Billing or Apple's in-app purchase system, depending on the user's platform. We do not receive or store users' payment card details or payment method information. The app receives only the information needed to verify purchases, restore purchases, and provide purchased content or features.

6. Notifications

Triple Riddle may send local reminder notifications if the user enables notifications. To provide this feature, the app may store notification preference status and the next reminder time locally on the device.

Users can disable notifications in the app settings or in the device system settings.

7. Local Data Storage

Some data is stored locally on the user's device, including game progress, statistics, settings, language preference, purchase state, token balance, sound settings, and notification preferences. This data is used to keep the app experience consistent between sessions.

Uninstalling the app usually removes locally stored app data from the device.

8. Sharing of Information

We do not sell users' personal data.

Data may be processed by the following third-party service providers:

  • Google Firebase Analytics, for app usage analytics.
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics, for crash reporting and diagnostics.
  • Google AdMob and Google User Messaging Platform, for advertising and consent management.
  • Google Play Billing and Apple in-app purchase services, for processing in-app purchases.

These providers process data according to their own privacy policies and terms.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

9. Data Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect user data. Data transmitted to third-party services is sent using secure connections. Access to data is limited to the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

10. Data Retention and Deletion

Local app data is stored on the user's device until the user deletes the app, clears app data, or resets progress inside the app where such a feature is available.

Data processed by Google and Apple services is retained according to the retention policies of the relevant services.

Users may request deletion or clarification of data related to Triple Riddle by contacting us at midisoft.game@gmail.com.

Because Triple Riddle does not use user accounts, in-app account deletion is not applicable.

11. Children

Triple Riddle is intended for users aged 13 and older and is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly ask children to provide personal contact information. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at midisoft.game@gmail.com, and we will take reasonable steps to delete such data.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when app features, third-party services, or legal requirements change. The current version will be available through the privacy policy link on the app's store listing and developer website.
