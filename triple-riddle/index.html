How it plays

Every level contains three simple word clues. Choose letters from the pool, fill the answers, and unlock the next clue when the open answers are solved.

Daily mode gives one available level at a time. Practice mode lets you keep playing through a larger question bank at your own pace.

Features

  • Daily and practice word puzzle modes.
  • Multiple supported languages.
  • Hints, rewarded ads, and optional token packs.
  • Progress, settings, sound, and notification controls.
