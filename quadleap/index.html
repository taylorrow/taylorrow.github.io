Quadleap

Connect four clues to one hidden answer. Quadleap mixes daily challenges, practice levels, hints, streaks, and multilingual word banks into quick puzzle sessions.

Privacy Policy Contact support

How it plays

Each level gives four clues that point to one answer. Use meaning, hints, and careful guesses to solve the connection before your attempts run out.

Daily mode gives one shared challenge per day. Practice mode lets you keep solving from a larger bank at your own pace.

Features

  • Daily and practice puzzle modes.
  • Multiple supported languages.
  • Optional hints, rewarded ads, and hint credits.
  • Progress, streaks, settings, sound, and notification controls.
