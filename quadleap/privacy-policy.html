Privacy Policy for Quadleap

Effective date: April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Quadleap handles information about users and their devices.

App developer: MidiSG
Privacy contact: midisoft.game@gmail.com

1. Information We Collect and Process

Quadleap does not require users to create an account and does not ask users to provide their name, phone number, address, or other direct contact information.

The app may process the following types of data:

  • App usage data, such as game starts, game completions, hint usage, settings changes, shop interactions, purchase restoration, and result sharing actions.
  • Crash and diagnostic data, such as crash reports, stack traces, app state at the time of a crash, device model, operating system version, and other technical information.
  • Device data and identifiers that may be processed by third-party SDKs, including advertising identifiers, app instance identifiers, IP address, device model, operating system version, and language settings.
  • Advertising data, including ad impressions, ad interactions, and data needed to display, measure, and improve ads.
  • Purchase-related data, such as purchase status, purchase identifiers, and information needed to provide purchased features. Payment details, such as card numbers, are processed by Google Play and are not received by Quadleap.
  • Local app data, such as game progress, statistics, settings, language preference, purchase state, hint balance, notification preferences, and reminder schedule.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's core features.
  • Save game progress, statistics, settings, and purchases.
  • Show ads and provide rewards for watching ads.
  • Process in-app purchases and restore previously purchased features.
  • Send local reminder notifications if the user enables notifications.
  • Analyze app usage and improve the user experience.
  • Diagnose crashes, fix bugs, and improve app stability.
  • Prevent abuse and keep services working correctly.

3. Advertising

Quadleap uses Google AdMob to display ads. Google AdMob may collect and process device information, advertising identifiers, IP address, ad interaction data, and other technical information needed to serve, personalize, limit the frequency of, and measure ads.

Users can manage ad personalization through their device settings and Google account settings.

More information about how Google uses data from apps and sites that use its services is available here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

4. Analytics and Crash Reporting

Quadleap uses Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used and Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports. These services may process technical device data, app usage events, crash information, and app instance identifiers.

This information helps us find issues, improve app stability, and understand which features may need improvement.

5. In-App Purchases

In-app purchases in Quadleap are processed through Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store users' payment card details or payment method information. The app receives only the information needed to verify purchases, restore purchases, and provide purchased content or features.

6. Notifications

Quadleap may send local reminder notifications if the user enables notifications. To provide this feature, the app may store notification preference status and the next reminder time locally on the device.

Users can disable notifications in the app settings or in the device system settings.

7. Local Data Storage

Some data is stored locally on the user's device, including game progress, statistics, settings, purchase state, and notification preferences. This data is used to keep the app experience consistent between sessions.

Uninstalling the app usually removes locally stored app data from the device.

8. Sharing of Information

We do not sell users' personal data.

Data may be processed by the following third-party service providers:

  • Google Firebase Analytics, for app usage analytics.
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics, for crash reporting and diagnostics.
  • Google AdMob, for advertising.
  • Google Play Billing, for processing in-app purchases.

These providers process data according to their own privacy policies and terms.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

9. Data Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect user data. Data transmitted to Google services is sent using secure connections. Access to data is limited to the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

10. Data Retention and Deletion

Local app data is stored on the user's device until the user deletes the app, clears app data, or resets progress inside the app where such a feature is available.

Data processed by Google services is retained according to the retention policies of the relevant Google services.

Users may request deletion or clarification of data related to Quadleap by contacting us at midisoft.game@gmail.com.

Because Quadleap does not use user accounts, in-app account deletion is not applicable.

11. Children

Quadleap is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly ask children to provide personal contact information. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at midisoft.game@gmail.com, and we will take reasonable steps to delete such data.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when app features, third-party services, or legal requirements change. The current version will be available through the privacy policy link on the app's Google Play listing.
